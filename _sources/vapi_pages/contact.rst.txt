Contact
=======

Developer discussions for the openvehicle-api project.

To post a message, send an email to: **openvehicle-api-dev@eclipse.org** 
Note: You must be subscribed to post.

Archives and RSS feed: https://www.eclipse.org/mailman/listinfo/openvehicle-api-dev

There is a bi weekly zoom meeting in the Eclipse SDV community calendar:

    Wednesdays at 12:00 CET
    ``Eclipse Open Vehicle API Bi-weekly Meeting``

Goto `SDV Community Calendar <https://calendar.google.com/calendar/newembed?src=c_2ampi2bmoka3qter4dceap1d5g@group.calendar.google.com&ctz=Europe/Berlin/>`_ and look for `Eclipse Open Vehicle API Bi-weekly Meeting` (Wednesdays)

https://eclipsesdv.org/get-involved/
